Introducing California's Greatest Cliffs

What is unique about California cliffs?

California cliff are one of a kind the United States where no cliff are as manificent and beautiful as them, And California is the only state that have cliffs that fronts all possible environment: sea, river, mountains and canyons. There are four such kinds indiviual unique cliffs. West Cliff, North Cliff, East Cliff and south Cliff. All offer fantastic overlooking views, recreational aras, camping sites, wildlifes watch sports and overnight cabins.

Where are these cliffs?

Located within a 100 mile radius from Irvine, California, they can be reached by roads easily. The closet cliff to Irvine is West Cliff, Which is the most popular and most visited.
